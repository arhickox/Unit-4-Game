Instructions

You will be given a goal number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals above. By clicking a crystal, you will add a specific amount to your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number.

You lose the game if your score goes above the goal number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the goal number and the values of each crystal will change.
